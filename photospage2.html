Four decades of family memories

THREE GENERATIONS of the Lynch family, including more dogs than they can quickly count, have called Lost Lure Lodge "home" for the summer since 1968.

Campfires, stargazing, cookouts, flashlight tag, blueberry picking and rainy-day charades are all the stuff of precious memories.

Kids have learned to swim, fish, paddle and sail on the peaceful waters of Belleau Lake. And adults and kids alike have lost more than a few fishing lures in the search for the "big one" over the years, which is how our little cottage got its name.
